## 2. Find a Pediatrician and Family Doctor

**For children:**
- Contact your health insurance for in-network pediatricians
- UNC Pediatrics and WakeMed Pediatrics have Bengali-speaking staff
- See [Community Directory](/directory) for specific recommendations

**For adults:**
- Establish care with a primary care physician within your first 3 months
- Get any chronic condition medications transferred to US pharmacy (Walgreens, CVS, Costco)

**Note on prescriptions:**  
Most South Asian medications have US equivalents. Bring a list of your current medications with generic names. CVS and Costco pharmacies are generally cheapest.

## 3. Long-Term Immigration Planning

**H-1B holders — start thinking about:**
- Green card sponsorship timeline (talk to your HR/employer)
- Priority date tracking: travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/legal/visa-law0/visa-bulletin.html
- Consult a Bangla-speaking immigration attorney early (see [Directory](/directory))

**Important documents to keep organized:**
- All I-797 approval notices
- All W-2s and tax returns
- Passport with visa stamps
- I-94 records
- Employment offer letters

---

## 4. File Your US Tax Return

**Deadline:** April 15 each year (for previous year income)

**Free filing options:**
- IRS Free File (if income < $73,000): irs.gov/freefile
- VITA (Volunteer Income Tax Assistance) — free help from IRS-trained volunteers
  - Locations in Raleigh, Durham, Cary each tax season
  - Search: irs.gov/vita

**Bangla-speaking CPAs:** See [Community Directory](/directory)

**Common deductions for new arrivals:**
- Student loan interest
- Moving expenses (if relocating for work — limited)
- Home office deduction (if working from home)

## 5. Halal Daycare Options

Finding halal or Muslim-friendly daycare can be challenging but options exist:

- **ICM has a weekend Islamic school** and can connect families with trusted home daycare providers
- **Sunshine Bangla Daycare** (Morrisville) — Bengali language environment (see [Directory](/directory))
- **Home-based daycare networks** in the Bangladeshi community — ask at TBSNC events or community WhatsApp groups
- Many Pakistani and Indian Muslim families in the area share daycare provider recommendations

## 6. Remittances Back Home

Sending money to Bangladesh:

| Service | Rate | Speed |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Wise (formerly TransferWise)** | Best rates | 1–2 days |
| **Remitly** | Good rates | Express: hours; Economy: days |
| **bKash partner apps** | Direct to bKash | Varies |
| **Western Union / MoneyGram** | Higher fees | Fast |

> Most services require identity verification on first use. Have your SSN, address, and bank account ready.

## 7. Long-Term: Buying a Home

Many Bangladeshis in RTP transition from renting to buying within 3–5 years.

**Popular neighborhoods for Bangladeshi families:**
- **Morrisville** — West Morrisville, Cambridge neighborhood
- **Cary** — Kildaire Farm area, Preston, MacGregor Downs
- **Apex** — newer construction, excellent schools

**Steps to prepare:**
1. Build 2 years of US credit history
2. Save for 20% down payment (avoid PMI)
3. Get pre-approved through your bank or a mortgage broker
4. Work with a community realtor (see [Directory](/directory))

---

## 8. Long-Term: Citizenship

After holding a Green Card for 5 years (3 years if married to a US citizen):

- Apply for naturalization (N-400 form)
- Must have been physically present in the US for at least 30 months of the 5 years
- Basic English and US history/civics test
- Consult an immigration attorney for specifics
