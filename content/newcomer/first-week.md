# Your First Week in RTP

Welcome to the Research Triangle! Here's what to do in your first 7 days to get settled quickly.

---

## 1. Get a Phone Number

**Same day priority.** You'll need a US phone number for almost everything.

- **T-Mobile** and **AT&T** have stores throughout Cary and Morrisville
- **Mint Mobile** (online, uses T-Mobile network) — cheapest option, ~$15/month
- **Visible** (Verizon network, $25/month unlimited) — great value
- For international calling back home, use **Google Voice** or **WhatsApp calling**

> **Tip:** Bring your passport. You don't need an SSN to get a prepaid SIM.

---

## 2. Open a Bank Account

You need a US bank account to get paid, pay rent, and build credit history.

**Banks that open accounts without SSN (passport + visa acceptable):**
- **Bank of America** — most accessible, many RTP branches
- **Chase** — good app, widely accepted
- **Wells Fargo** — large branch network in the Triangle

**Locations near you:**
- Bank of America: 1000 Preston Executive Dr, Cary (near ICM)
- Chase: 101 SW Cary Pkwy, Cary

> **Note:** Bring passport + visa + employer offer letter. Some branches may require a second form of ID.

---

## 3. Get Your Social Security Number (SSN)

**Wait 10 business days after entry** before applying — the SSA system needs time to receive your entry record from DHS.

**Social Security Administration Office (Raleigh):**  
📍 3220 Croasdaile Dr, Durham, NC 27705  
📞 1-800-772-1213  
🕐 Mon–Fri 9am–4pm (arrive early — lines get long)

**What to bring:**
- Passport
- I-94 (print from i94.cbp.dhs.gov)
- Visa (F-1, H-1B, etc.)
- Employment Authorization (if applicable)
- Offer letter or I-20 (for students)

> **SSN Timeline:** Typically takes 2–4 weeks to arrive by mail. Do NOT request expedited delivery.

---

## 4. Find Temporary Housing

If you don't have permanent housing yet:

**Extended Stay options near RTP:**
- Extended Stay America (multiple locations in Cary/Morrisville)
- WoodSpring Suites, Morrisville
- Many community members offer short-term room rentals — ask in TBSNC or local Facebook groups

**Facebook groups to join:**
- "Bangladeshi Community RTP NC"
- "Bangla RTP Housing & Roommates"
- "Triangle Bangladeshi Community"

---

## 5. Get a Google Voice Number (Optional)

While waiting for your phone plan, get a free Google Voice number for US calls and texts using WiFi. Useful for job applications and apartment hunting.

---

## 6. Introduction to the Community

**Join these groups immediately:**
- **TBSNC** (Triangle Bangladeshi Society of NC) — main community org
- **ICM (Islamic Center of Morrisville)** — Friday prayers, community events
- **BSA@NCSU** (if you're an NC State student)
- Local WhatsApp groups (ask at ICM or through TBSNC)
